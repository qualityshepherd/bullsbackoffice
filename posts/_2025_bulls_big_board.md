**Bulls Tier**
1. [Rasheer Fleming](https://www.tankathon.com/players/rasheer-fleming) 20.9yo (170 days older than DQ), athletic dunky/blocky/shooty with wingspan for days; great D; 39% from 3 on 4 attempts; a fucking LOAD; switchable 4/5... BULLS-TAKE: this is my guy if we pick 10th or 15th. Will anchor the defense and maybe DPOY potential
1. [Thomas Sorber](https://www.tankathon.com/players/thomas-sorber) XL block machine; D looks good; good feet; switchable; less fluid than Rasheer but BIG. Maybe 10 lbs too heavy? Can't shoot yet.
1. [Noa Essengue](https://www.tankathon.com/players/noa-essengue) young, SKINNY 6'10 athletic F; nice bag; good instincts; can pass; really creative finisher. Gonna need at LEAST 15lbs. Shot (30%) is slow but not horrible. Good defender... I like everything about this kid he pops in film... you notice him a LOT
1. [Derik Queen](https://www.tankathon.com/players/derik-queen) SOFT hands scorer; passing; handle; touch... D should be better. BULLS-TAKE: he shows flashes of disruptive D... if that ever becomes consistent, this kid's a monster. Otherwise I don't love his fit on the Bulls but I like his value. He's a star
1. [Cedric Coward](https://www.tankathon.com/players/cedric-coward) older wing that is a fucking ATHLETE. Wingspan! Can shoot, pressure the rim and a decent passer
1. [Carter Bryant](https://www.tankathon.com/players/carter-bryant)6'8 wing; athletic finisher that can put it on the floor. Decent shooting; great D; bit of a shot blocker; limited rebounding. He's good not great
1. [Danny Wolf](https://www.tankathon.com/players/danny-wolf) will be a quality backup C, especially if he can shoot 40% from 3
1. [Asa Newell](https://www.tankathon.com/players/asa-newell) tall bouncy baller; good D; he's a center; needs 10lbs and work on his shot but this is a guy BULLS-TAKE: Rasheer is better and will fit better
1. [Nique Clifford](https://www.tankathon.com/players/nique-clifford) - old but a do-it-all wing; some hops, can shoot; good D; nice player. BULLS-TAKE: not a _need_... but a baller
1. [Joan Beringer](https://www.tankathon.com/players/joan-beringer) 6'10, 230; mobile rim runner; nice footwork; mostly non-shooter (0 from 3%; 570% ft) but CAN hit an open look inside the arc. I like the defense... can stay in front; decent shot blocker. JT Toppin

At least one of these guys will be a Bull this year... it's anyone's guess which. If I had to bet _TODAY_, I'm betting Cedric Coward? He fills a real need at the wing, is a sexy pick and looks to have serious upside.
